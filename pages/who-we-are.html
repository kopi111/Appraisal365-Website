Our Firm

TEN24 Realty is an integrated built-environment services firm where our model was specifically designed to satisfy the ever growing niche of having authentic and reliable Appraisal, Surveyor's ID Reports and Construction/Bill of Quantity Estimates prepared at globally accepted standard, in record turn-around-times, without the compromise of quality.

Our key partners are seasoned and licensed professionals, whom have extensive hands-on, day to day knowledge and experience in our major service sectors.

At TEN24 Realty, we are dedicated to providing customer service and client satisfaction of the highest standard, and we take dear pride in working for the benefit of our Clientele, as we bring the luxury of affordability to all, without compromising quality in our product delivery.

Our Mission

To achieve the highest standards in appraisal and complementary real estate services, by utilizing best practice technologies to ensure excellent customer satisfaction, retention and long-term relationships.

Our Vision

Engender value added change in the appraisal and real estate market segment, through products and services that guarantee superior quality, speed and precision for all.

Our Core Values

Professionalism, Trustworthiness, Integrity, Confidentiality, Respect & Responsive Service.
